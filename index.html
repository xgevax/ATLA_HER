The world of Avatar the last airbender

A short introduction

Avatar The Last Airbender is a show that is very dear to my heart. I watched it growing up and still watch it now as an (almost) twenty year old. Avatar taught me a lot about caring for others, friendships, forgiveness and letting go in order to move on.

No matter how old you are it's a story that will hit you right in the feels because it's so heavy (especially for a childrens show). The story is mostly about war and grief but somehow also manages to be funny and heartwarming.

After Aang wakes up from being frozen in a major chunk of ice for about 100 years, he quickly learns about the war and how it destroyed everything he once knew.

The series follows Avatar Aang and his friends on their rocky journey to defeating Fire Lord Ozai.
